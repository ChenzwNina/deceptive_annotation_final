Question 1: Can you describe how the widget looks, such as its colors, size of elements, and layout? Why do you make these design choices?

    The widget is a clean, rectangular banner with rounded corners, positioned at the top of the screen. It has a white background with a subtle border and shadow to make it stand out from the page content. The layout is a horizontal flexbox, organizing content from left to right: an icon, the text, action buttons, and a close button. On smaller screens, it stacks vertically for better readability.

    Colors: The palette is professional and trustworthy. A deep blue (#0052cc) is used for the primary "Join Now" button and the icon to draw attention to the main actions without being aggressive. Text colors are dark grey (#172b4d) for high readability. Secondary elements use neutral greys.

    Elements: The headline is bold and slightly larger than the descriptive paragraph. The buttons are equally sized to provide a clear, non-manipulative choice. An 'X' button is clearly visible for easy dismissal.

    These choices create a calm, informative, and user-respecting interface. The design is intended to be helpful, not intrusive, clearly communicating the value proposition and cost without pressure.
