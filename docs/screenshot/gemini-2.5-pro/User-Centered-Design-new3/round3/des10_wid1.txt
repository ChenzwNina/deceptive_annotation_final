    Question 2: What triggers the appearance of this widget?

    The appearance of this widget should be triggered by user engagement rather than appearing immediately upon page load. An immediate popup can be jarring and disrupt the user's browsing intent.

    A suitable trigger would be behavioral, such as when a user:
    1. Scrolls 50-60% down a product or category page. This indicates they have shown interest in the site's content.
    2. Navigates to their second or third page within a single session. This signals a deeper level of engagement than a casual, one-page visit.
    3. Spends a specific amount of time on the site, for example, 30 seconds.

    This approach respects the user's initial experience (supporting autonomy and control), feels less intrusive, and presents the membership offer at a moment when the user is more likely to see its value, thus increasing the chance of a positive interaction.
